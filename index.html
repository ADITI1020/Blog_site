UNSTOPPABLE

A sky full of stars
A head full of dreams
Suppressed by society
Left to scream.

Wanted to fly
They cut my wings
Found a love
They broke my limbs.

They say everything in my life
is preordained
"Am I so bad mom?"
asked I in vain.

To fly high, to touch the sky
To study hard, to get a life
Was it all too much
For a GIRL to achieve?

They shred my wings
They broke my limbs
Because I had a vision
I had a dream.

Still standing strong
Still standing firm
Because
Like a PHOENIX I'm meant to fight
I'm meant to rise.

|-APOORVA SINGH.
