Key to a Happy Life
Lifestyle Gadget
Apoorva Singh

11 JULY, 2020 11:20 am

One fine day, I was sitting and wondering, what exactly happiness means to me. I’ve always had this in mind that happiness is something that you feel when situations go just as you want it to. But, that day after thinking a lot about it for a few hours I realized, that’s not what actual happiness is!

No matter how blessed you are, no matter how wealthy you are, you’ll never be happy if you haven’t learned to cross the path of Self-love. All the criticisms and judgements from negative people will stop affecting you when you have acknowledged your purpose in life. You’ll think of them as yet another motivator and not a hater! Clichéd but true.

Happiness comes from within. When you have accepted yourself entirely, you feel a sense of pride, you feel as if you’ve achieved something. That is happiness! It doesn’t always have to come from materialistic things. Materialistic happiness never lasts. I read somewhere, “It is not how much we have, but how much we enjoy, that determines happiness.” Cherish what you have because there are plenty of people who are not as privileged as you. Even though you consider yourself unlucky, under privileged, you are still above thousands of people. Chase your dreams, chase your goals. Stop complaining, start living.

Everyday when you wake up, ask three questions to yourself, “Who are you?” “What is your purpose in life?” “Are you genuinely happy?” and keep repeating it until you actually find answers to all the three questions. Sometimes, it’s necessary to remind yourself that you’re the truest version of you and no matter what anyone thinks, they’ll never get what you have, they’ll never be who you are. When you are determined and motivated enough, it’s then you can find the most important key in your life – The Key to Happiness. And once you find that, the door to success will automatically open.
