You Decide who you are
Lifestyle Gadget
Apoorva Singh

11 JULY, 2020 11:20 am

It took me many years to realise that I had given more power to people to have a say in my life than I gave myself and it took me many years to realise that it was my choice. I was the one allowing them to invade my mind. I was responsible for them invading my space. I allowed them to distort my thoughts with their noise. When you give someone the key to your life, you give them the power to drain you mentally. You give them the power to “ruin” you.

I firmly believe that no one knows you more than you do, no one understands you more than you do and no one knows your potential more than you do. Even if they are older, or more experienced, or more successful, they don’t know what you need more than you do but if you keep giving people your power, they will eventually inhibit you. That’s all I ever felt when I gave them more power; inhibited.

And I realized nothing feels worse than pretending what you’re not, being someone that you are not just to keep people around you. People will come and go, no matter what you do. At the end, only you stay with your own self. No one else does. Nothing feels worse than losing yourself in the process of pleasing others. Nothing feels worse than being stuck in a life you didn’t choose for yourself. And believe me when I say, you can give the world to people and they’ll still have the audacity to question what you did for them.

The good news is, nothing feels better than knowing you can change it all. You are always one choice/decision away from a new life, from a new beginning . You’re always one choice away from a new you. The you that has been stifled by the judgements and words of others, your family, or your friends. The you that has been trying to get out of the cocoon and breathe. Nothing feels better than knowing that you created an amazing life for yourself and it was your choice.
